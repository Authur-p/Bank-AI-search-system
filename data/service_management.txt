Service Management & Operations — Wema Bank (Expanded)

Service Philosophy:
Wema Bank focuses on delivering reliable, timely, and customer-centric services. Service management covers availability, incident handling, change management, capacity planning, and service-level commitments to customers.

Key Domains:
1. Incident Management:
- Rapid detection and response to service outages and customer-impacting incidents.
- Clear escalation paths, incident logging, and post-incident reviews to prevent recurrence.

2. Change & Release Management:
- Controlled rollout of new features and fixes, including testing, staging, and rollback plans.
- Communication plans for customers when significant service changes or downtime are expected.

3. Capacity & Performance Management:
- Monitoring of infrastructure to ensure transaction volumes are supported without degradation.
- Seasonal and promotional load planning to avoid outages.

4. Customer Experience & Feedback:
- Channels: In-app chat, phone support, email, and social media monitoring.
- Continuous improvement via customer feedback loops, surveys, and NPS tracking.

5. Business Continuity & Disaster Recovery:
- Backup systems, failover data centers, and runbooks for critical services.
- Regular drills and supplier resilience assessments to ensure continuity during major incidents.

Operational best practices:
- Maintain up-to-date runbooks and on-call rosters.
- Automate monitoring and alerting for abnormal behaviors and thresholds.
- Ensure clear communication to customers during incidents — transparent timings and remediation guidance.
