Support Channels:
- In-app chat: Real-time assistance for many common issues; escalation to human agents when required.
- Phone: Dedicated contact centers with hours listed by region; emergency hotlines for card or account compromise.
- Email & web forms: For non-urgent requests, complaints, and documentation uploads.
- Social media: Monitoring for service issues and public updates (useful for outage notices).

What to provide when reporting issues:
- Transaction reference number, date/time, and affected account to speed investigation.
- Screenshots or error messages when possible.
- Proof of identity when escalating sensitive issues.

Common support scenarios & tips:
- Lost/stolen card: Immediately lock via app or call support to hotlist the card; follow up with a written dispute if fraudulent charges occur.
- Unauthorized transaction: File a dispute and provide any evidence; keep communication records and follow up on investigation timelines.
- Account access issues: Verify device and app versions, check for maintenance windows, and confirm credentials. Consider resetting via official channels if required.
- Loan or account queries: Have loan reference, repayment schedule, or account statements ready to streamline support.
- Feedback & complaints: Use official complaint forms to ensure the matter is tracked and resolved within service timelines. Ask for a complaint reference number.

Availability & SLAs:
- Response times vary by channel and severity; critical account compromise issues receive immediate attention while standard queries follow published turnaround times.
- Keep contact details updated to ensure alerts and OTPs reach you during investigations.

Security reminder:
- The bank will never request full PINs, passwords, or OTPs through chat or phone calls. Always verify official contact points before sharing sensitive details.
