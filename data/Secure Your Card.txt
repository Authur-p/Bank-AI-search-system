Secure Your Card



Our priority is to provide you with a safe, secure, and convenient way to shop online with your Wema Bank Visa Card or MasterCard. Verified by Visa or MasterCard, Secure Code are services that help prevent unauthorized use of your card online. The extra layer of security helps prevent fraudulent purchases at the point of checkout before funds can be removed from your account. Now, you can start shopping online with peace of mind.
